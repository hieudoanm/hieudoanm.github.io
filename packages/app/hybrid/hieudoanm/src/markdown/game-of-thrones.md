## Seven Kingdoms

Historically, before Aegon I Targaryen united Westeros, there were seven
independent kingdoms:

| No  | Kingdom                              | Ruling House (at Conquest) | Capital / Seat |
| --- | ------------------------------------ | -------------------------- | -------------- |
| 1   | Kingdom of the North                 | Stark                      | Winterfell     |
| 2   | Kingdom of the Mountain and the Vale | Arryn                      | The Eyrie      |
| 3   | Kingdom of the Isles and the Rivers  | Hoare                      | Harrenhal      |
| 4   | Kingdom of the Rock                  | Lannister                  | Casterly Rock  |
| 5   | Kingdom of the Reach                 | Gardener                   | Highgarden     |
| 6   | Storm Kingdom                        | Durrandon                  | Storm's End    |
| 7   | Principality of Dorne\*              | Martell                    | Sunspear       |

- Dorne joined later through marriage rather than conquest.
